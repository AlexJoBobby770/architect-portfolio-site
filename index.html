Building Homes. Building Legacy.

Thomas Legacy Properties, LLC is a Georgia-based real estate investment and development company focused on residential projects in Lawrenceville and Gwinnett County.

Founded in honor of my late father, Thomas, we are committed to disciplined real estate investing, quality development, and giving back to the community through education-focused charitable support.

What We Focus On

  • Residential real estate investment
  • Single-family home development
  • Long-term value creation

About Us

Thomas Legacy Properties, LLC was established to honor the legacy of Thomas and the values he stood for—integrity, responsibility, and long-term vision.

With hands-on experience in residential real estate and construction, and a family background in the real estate industry, the company focuses on thoughtful residential investment and development in Georgia.

We work with licensed local professionals to ensure quality execution, transparency, and compliance.

Our goal is not just to build properties—but to build a lasting legacy for future generations.

What We Do

Real Estate Investment

We acquire and own residential real estate with a focus on long-term value, strong fundamentals, and responsible growth.

Real Estate Development

We develop single-family residential properties, including new construction and renovation projects, primarily in Lawrenceville and surrounding areas.

Property Management

We manage company-owned properties only, ensuring quality maintenance and tenant satisfaction.

Giving Back

Thomas Legacy Education Initiative

At Thomas Legacy Properties, LLC, we believe success includes giving back to the community. In honor of my late father, Thomas, we support education-focused charitable organizations that help children and students access better opportunities.

A portion of company profits is donated annually to registered educational charities. This initiative reflects our belief in ethical business practices, community impact, and long-term responsibility.
